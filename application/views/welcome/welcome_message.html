Criticality Analysis of Diabetic Gait in Children

OUR MISSION

CARDIGAN project aims to use criticality analysis of gait to identify individuals at risk of developing diabetes among overweight teenagers in Mexico. This project drew together computer scientists, life scientists and clinicians from Mexico and UK and can significantly change the way that diabetes is detected. Read more...
